About Me

I am a fifth-year Architecture student at the University of Cyprus with a strong focus on architectural visualization as a tool for design thinking and communication. I am driven by the challenge of translating architectural concepts into precise, atmospheric, and meaningful visual narratives.

With a critical eye for drawing, spatial composition, and structural logic, I develop architecture and visualization projects that balance conceptual clarity with visual impact. My work explores the intersection of design intent and representation, using visualization not only to present ideas, but to refine and test them throughout the design process.
